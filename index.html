About Me


My name is Nick Moore, I moved to Denver in 2008 to finish my BS degree at Rocky Mountain College of Art and Design. I first came out to Denver when I was 12 years old and knew that I needed to live here one day. And now I do! Yay! I live here with my wife, Kate and our fur baby, Beaux. I am currently a graphic designer, and looking to make a change in career to web developement and this course is the first step in that goal.

When I'm not working I like to watch movies, play video games (console), drink beer, and do typical Colorado stuff (hiking, snowboarding, camping). I am very excited to be in this class and very excited to get to know and work with everyone! Lets learn the things! ALL THE THINGS!!!!
